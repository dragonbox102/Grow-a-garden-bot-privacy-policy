🌿 Grow a Garden Stocks – Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy enclave of Grow a Garden Stocks (“the Bot” or “the Service”). This document outlines the data practices employed by the Bot in strict alignment with our operational ethos of minimal data retention, complete transparency, and absolute respect for your privacy within the digital garden.

By using the Bot within your Discord server, you acknowledge and consent to the data collection and usage practices described herein.

1. 🌱 Guiding Principles

  • Minimal Data, Maximum Functionality: We only collect what is strictly necessary for the Bot to function effectively within your Discord server.
  • No Personal Information Harvesting: We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII) from individual users.
  • Local-Only Data Retention: All data is stored locally on the Bot's operational environment — no third-party cloud providers, external databases, or remote servers.
  • Transparency & Accountability: We maintain full transparency over what is stored, why it’s stored, and how it’s used.

2. 🧩 Data We Collect

a. Server Configuration Data

  • Server (Guild) ID: Required for identifying where the Bot operates.
  • Channel IDs: Where to send alerts, events, and updates.
  • Setup Status: Boolean that shows if the Bot is configured.
  • Bot Admin Roles:
    • Role ID and Name – For granting special permissions.
    • Admin User ID – The Discord ID of the user who added the role.
    • Timestamp – When the role was added for tracking and audit purposes.

b. Temporary and Runtime Data

  • Manual Stock Data: Empty unless configured manually.
  • Manual Weather Data: Optional override for weather events.
  • Maintenance Flags: Temporary indicator for downtime mode.
  • Backup Files: Used for recovery and data integrity.

3. 🔒 What We Don't Collect

  • Store user messages or command inputs.
  • Collect usernames, emails, or display names.
  • Track direct messages or private Discord communications.
  • Transmit any data to external services, APIs (besides Discord’s), or analytics platforms.

The only stored user-linked data is the Discord User ID of the admin who adds Bot roles — and even that is for server-side auditing only.

4. 🔁 How We Use This Data

  • Maintain Bot configuration per server.
  • Send accurate notifications to designated channels.
  • Secure command access via authorized admin roles.
  • Support diagnostics, audit trails, and backup recovery.
  • Improve uptime and operational behavior during maintenance periods.

5. 📦 Data Storage & Retention

  • All data resides in locally stored JSON files.
  • Backup files are timestamped and used for disaster recovery only.
  • Ephemeral files (e.g., maintenance flags) are automatically deleted after use.
  • No data is shared, sold, or exposed to any third party — ever.

6. 🚫 Data Sharing Policy

  • We do not sell or rent data.
  • We do not use third-party trackers or ad services.
  • We do not export data externally unless explicitly required by law (which has never happened).

7. 🔄 Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may evolve as the Bot improves. Any updates will be reflected on the official documentation or announcement channels. Continued usage of the Bot implies acceptance of the updated policy.

Final Notes

Grow a Garden Stocks is a privacy-first, non-commercial utility designed to enhance your in-server gardening stock journey — with no surveillance, no hidden data siphons, and no nonsense.

By continuing to use the Bot, you agree to this Privacy Policy in full.

Enjoy cultivating your digital garden in peace. 🌿
